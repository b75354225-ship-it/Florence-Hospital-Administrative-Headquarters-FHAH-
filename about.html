Our Story

Serving Kampala for Over 25 Years

Florence Hospital Administrative Headquarters (FHAH) was founded with a simple goal: to make high-quality, patient-centered healthcare accessible to every family in our community. What began as a small clinic has grown into a full-service hospital with specialists across every major department.

Today, FHAH combines modern medical technology, internationally recognized practices and a deeply compassionate team to deliver care patients can trust.

Our Mission

To improve the health and well-being of every patient through safe, affordable and reliable healthcare, delivered with dignity and respect.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted healthcare institution — recognized for clinical excellence, innovation and compassionate patient care.
