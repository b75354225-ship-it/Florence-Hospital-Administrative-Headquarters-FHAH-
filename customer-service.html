Common Questions

How do I reschedule an appointment?+

Submit a support ticket or call us with your appointment reference and preferred new time.

My payment didn't go through — what now?+

Submit a ticket with your invoice number and payment method used. We'll confirm the status within one business day.

How long does a support ticket take?+

Most tickets are answered within a few hours; urgent medical concerns should go through the emergency hotline.
