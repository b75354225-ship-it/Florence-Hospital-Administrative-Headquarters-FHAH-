FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment for outpatient care?+

Walk-ins are welcome for OPD, but booking ahead reduces your wait time. Use our Appointments page to request a slot.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Mobile money, major debit/credit cards, bank transfer and insurance. Visit the Payments page to settle a bill online.

Can I get my lab results online?+

Contact Customer Service with your patient number and our lab team will advise on secure result delivery.

How do I make a donation to the hospital?+

Visit our Donate page for mobile money, bank transfer and card giving options.
